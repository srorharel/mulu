MULU - Car air freshener artwork (print ready)
==============================================

FILES
-----
mulu-freshener-print-ready.pdf   Page 1 = FRONT (logo), Page 2 = BACK (QR).
                                 Sized with bleed; trim box is embedded in the PDF.
print-front-green.png            Front artwork.   898 x 1252 px @ 300 DPI.
print-back.png                   Back artwork.    898 x 1252 px @ 300 DPI.
qr-mulu-clean.svg                The QR code as vector (scales to any size, stays sharp).

SPECS
-----
Finished (trim) size : 70 x 100 mm
Design size (bleed)  : 76 x 106 mm   (3 mm bleed on every side)
Resolution           : 300 DPI
Safe margin          : keep logo / text / QR at least 6 mm inside the trim
QR code              : ~30 mm on the back -- DO NOT crop the white border around it,
                       and keep it on white (never print the dark squares on green)
Hanging hole         : leave the top ~12-15 mm clear for the string hole
Brand green          : #26B55F

NOTES
-----
- The QR has been scan-tested; it opens https://muluwash.com
- Please print ONE sample and scan it before the full run.
- If you use a different template/size, ask for re-exported files.
